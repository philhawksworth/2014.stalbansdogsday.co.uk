Stalls, refreshments and events will run throughout the afternoon. We'll have a hog roast, ice creams, great teas coffee from Little Soko coffee and plenty of other refreshments.

Competitions

Anyone is eligible to enter a dog into a competition. Competitions will include

  • Most improved rescue dog
  • Golden oldie (dogs over 7 years)
  • Junior Handler (under 16 - minimumum of 5 commands)
  • Longest sit and stay
  • Best trick
  • Speed call (fastest sit and stay)
  • Dog most like its owner
  • Best ball / biscuit catcher
  • Judges wants to take home

Competition registration opens at 12:30pm.
